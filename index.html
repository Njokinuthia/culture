THINGS TO DO IN NAIROBI

As a first timer in Nairobi, it can feel abit hectic, big and intimidating at times.

But once you spend some time in the city you get to realise Nairobi is full of social activities, lots of art and culture and a great exciting night life scene.

You just need to know the right places. I am going to give you ideas for some of the best things to do in the capital of Kenya

List of things to do

  1. Visit Nairobi National Park

    2. Its a short drive out of Nairobi's central business district.

  2. Shop at Maasai market

    3. This is an open air market where you can find a variety of cultural themed artifacts like

    • clothes and fabric
    • sisal bags
    • wood carvings
    • beaded necklaces and jewelries
    • drawings and paintings

    The Maasai Market is open several days a week at different locations in Nairobi from 8am to 6pm.

    • Monday -Closed
    • Tuesday -Kijabe Street opposite the Norfolk Hotel
    • Wednesday -Capital Center on Mombasa Road
    • Thursday -The Junction Mall on Ngong Road
    • Friday -Village Market on Limuru Road
    • Saturday -Nairobi Law Courts parking in the City Center
    • Sunday -Yaya Center on Argwings Kodhek Road

  3. Enjoy a nature walk in Karura Forest

    4. If you want to be in a serene environment or take in nature at its finest Karura forest is the place to visit.List of activities to engage in there include:

    • Bike rides
    • Nature trails
    • Picnics
    • Scenic waterfalls

  4. Visit night clubs

    5. Nairobi boasts of it's exotic exciting night life. Go clubbing with a few friends on r la Friday night and have the time of your life.

    For starters you can try out these clubs:

    • Mercury Lounge
    • The Alchemist Bar
    • Brew Bistro
    • Tribeka
